Features

Create

Create choose-your own adventure style stories and games easily with Fantasy Makers graphical page creation tool. Create complex stories with many different paths and pages by seamlessly linking pages together graphically and adding control logic that will change how players interact with the game based on their player stats and attributes that you can define yourself. Fantasy Maker makes creating games easy!


Play

Once you've created your game, feel free to give it a spin in all without leaving the browser. FantasyMaker provides the functionality to play your games or games your friends have created and gives you the tools to customise how each page will appear to players.


Share

When completed, we hope to provide an online community for FantasyMaker's to come together and share their games. FantasyMaker will allow you to easily share URL's to your games, collaborate on game creation and browse weekly and monthly top games all voted on by the FantasyMaker Community.
