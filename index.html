Introducing FantasyMaker

Making interactive storytelling easier

View Project

Create

Easily create and edit choose-your-own-adventure style stories by creating pages and linking them seamlessly through FantasyMaker's graphical story creation page. Create complex custom stories and games without writing a single line of code!

Play

Once you've created your game give it a spin. FantasyMaker makes it easy; you never have to leave the browser to play your games. You can also browse and play stories and games created by other FantasyMakers!

Share

FantasyMaker will make it easy to share games you've created, just send your game's URL to a friend and they can play instantly. We also hope to include a web based community for sharing and rating FantasyMaker games and stories.
